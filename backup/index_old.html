About

Who I am

I'm Asad Ali, a Cyber Security student with a hands-on interest in ethical hacking, Linux and secure web development. I like understanding a system well enough to know where it's likely to fail — and then either fixing that, or building something that doesn't have the same problem.

Outside of coursework, I practice on CTF-style challenges, tinker with Kali Linux, and build small web projects to keep my front-end skills sharp. My goal is to grow into a professional penetration tester / security engineer.
